Why Should I Start a Discount Clothing Store Now?

Why now? Because now is the best time to take control of your destiny, become the boss and gain your financial independence.

Really any time of year is a great time to start a clothing business. The retail clothing industry revolves around cycles that repeat every year beginning with the back to school season and following through to the holiday seasons. In between the seasons are common events like high school proms, graduation parties, and of course swimsuit season. On top of these seasons and common events there are birthdays, parties, weddings, and more. As important as focusing on these seasons, special occasions and events it is even more vital that the store owner is prepared for them and react properly and on-time with a pre-emptive schedule that keeps the store’s inventory ahead of the curve. The Holiday seasons will generate on average 30% of a clothing store’s sales for the year! However, no matter when the store owner opens their doors there is a season to immediately take advantage of – which accounts for the other 70% of the year. Therefore, it is best that the new store owner begin the process as soon as possible in order to solidify public awareness and get their marketing plan in place.
